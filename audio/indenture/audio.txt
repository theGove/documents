This document is the form used to bind Isaiah Thomas to his master Zechariah Foul. It was the standard form used by the overseers of the poor in Suffolk County. Although many of the terms can be negotiated between master and servant, such as whether the servant is allowed to contract marriage during the term of the indenture, others were non-negotiable. Such as...   "Taverns, ale-houses, or places of gaming he shall not haunt or frequent"
