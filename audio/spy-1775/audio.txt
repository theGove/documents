Published by Isaiah Thomas, the Massachusetts Spy was the most popular newspaper in North America.  A large newspaper in the colonies might have 600 to 1000 subscribers.  By 1776, the Massachusetts Spy has more than 3,500 and it is read throughout the colonies.  

This copy was printed May 3, 1775.  It contains the first published eye-witness account of the Battle of Lexington alongside the other news of the day.  Purchase this newspaper to find out who fired first on the Lexington green.  Was it the British soldiers or was it the Lexington Militia?
